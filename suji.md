# Privacy Policy

**Suji** is a multi-variant Sudoku puzzle game for iOS. It is free to play with optional in-app purchases and ads. This policy explains what data Suji collects and how it is used.

## Data Collection

- Suji does **not** collect any personal information
- Suji does **not** require an account or registration
- Suji does **not** upload your game data to any server
- All game progress (puzzles, statistics, settings) is stored locally on your device

## Advertising

Suji displays ads provided by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

- Device identifiers (IDFA, when authorized via App Tracking Transparency)
- IP address (for approximate location-based ad targeting)
- App usage data (ad impressions, interactions)

You can opt out of personalized ads by declining the App Tracking Transparency prompt when the app first launches, or by adjusting your device settings under **Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking**.

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## In-App Purchases

Suji offers two one-time purchases:

- **Remove Ads** — permanently disables banner and interstitial ads
- **Theme Bundle** — unlocks 9 additional color themes

These transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Purchase validation is handled by **RevenueCat**, which may process anonymous transaction receipts. Suji does not collect or store any payment information.

For more information, see [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/).

## App Tracking Transparency

Suji requests your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before allowing any ad-related tracking. If you decline, only non-personalized ads will be shown. You can change this at any time in your device settings.

## Third-Party Services

Suji uses the following third-party services:

- **Google AdMob** — ad serving ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **RevenueCat** — in-app purchase management ([Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/))
- **Apple StoreKit** — in-app purchases (handled entirely by Apple)
- **SKAdNetwork** — privacy-preserving ad attribution by Apple

## Children's Privacy

Suji does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is rated 4+ and does not serve targeted ads to users who have declined tracking.

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
