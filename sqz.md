# Privacy Policy

**Sqz** is an image converter and compressor app for macOS. It processes images entirely on your Mac. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or images to external servers.

## Data Collection

- Sqz does **not** collect any personal information
- Sqz does **not** upload your images or any data to any server
- Sqz does **not** require an account or registration
- Sqz's App Store privacy label is **"Data Not Collected"**

## How Image Processing Works

Sqz uses on-device image encoding libraries to convert and compress your images. All processing happens locally on your Mac. Sqz **cannot** and does **not** send your images, thumbnails, or any visual data off your device.

## File Access

Sqz requires access to folders you select for reading source images and saving compressed output. This access is:

- Scoped to your explicit selections only
- Managed through macOS sandbox security-scoped bookmarks
- Never used to access files outside your chosen folders

## Local Data

All app data remains on your device:

- **User preferences** (output format, quality, output folder) are stored in `UserDefaults` on your device
- **Bookmarked folders** are stored as security-scoped bookmarks for persistent access to your chosen output directory
- **Purchase status** is managed by Apple's App Store (see below)

## In-App Purchases

Sqz offers a one-time in-app purchase managed through Apple's App Store and StoreKit:

- Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple's secure payment system
- Sqz does **not** process or store any payment information
- Sqz does **not** use any third-party payment or subscription SDK

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Apple StoreKit | In-app purchase management | None (handled by Apple) |

No other third-party SDKs or services are used. Sqz does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

## Children's Privacy

Sqz does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
