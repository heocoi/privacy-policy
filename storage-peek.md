# Privacy Policy

**Storage Peek** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection

- No personal information collected
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No network connections
- No third-party services

## Local Data

Storage metrics are read directly from your Mac's file system and displayed locally. No data leaves your device.

## Notifications

Low storage alerts are generated and displayed locally using macOS system notifications. No notification data is sent externally.
