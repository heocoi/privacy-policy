# Privacy Policy

**ExifStrip** is a photo metadata viewer, editor, and stripper for iOS. All processing happens entirely on your device. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or photos to external servers.

## Data Collection

- ExifStrip does **not** collect any personal information
- ExifStrip does **not** upload your photos or metadata to any server
- ExifStrip does **not** require an account or registration
- ExifStrip does **not** include analytics, crash reporting, or tracking SDKs
- ExifStrip's App Store privacy label is **"Data Not Collected"**

## How It Works

ExifStrip reads EXIF, IPTC, TIFF, and GPS metadata from your photos using Apple's ImageIO framework entirely on-device. When you strip or edit metadata, the modified photo is saved locally to your Photo Library or shared via the system share sheet. No data leaves your device.

## Photo Library Access

ExifStrip requires access to your Photo Library to:

- Read photo metadata (EXIF, GPS, IPTC, camera settings)
- Display photo thumbnails within the app
- Save stripped or edited copies to your library

ExifStrip never modifies your original photos. Stripped copies are saved as new files.

## Purchases

ExifStrip offers an optional one-time Pro purchase through Apple's App Store. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment information. RevenueCat processes an anonymous app user ID to manage entitlements - no personal data is collected.

## Local Data

All app data remains on your device:

- Strip history and preferences are stored in local app storage
- No cloud sync, no remote database, no server calls
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data

## Network Usage

ExifStrip works fully offline. The only network calls are:

- Validating purchases through RevenueCat (anonymous, no personal data)
- No other network activity

## Third-Party Services

- **RevenueCat**: Purchase validation only. [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/)

## Children's Privacy

ExifStrip does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.
