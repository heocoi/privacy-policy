# Privacy Policy

**Battery Vitals** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection

- No personal information collected
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No network connections
- No third-party services

## Local Data

Battery metrics are read directly from your Mac's hardware and displayed locally. No data leaves your device.
