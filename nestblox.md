# Privacy Policy

**Nestblox** is a casual block puzzle game for iOS. It is a free-to-play game with optional in-app purchases and ads. This policy explains what data Nestblox collects and how it is used.

## Data Collection

- Nestblox does **not** collect any personal information
- Nestblox does **not** require an account or registration
- Nestblox does **not** upload your game data to any server
- All game progress (scores, settings) is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults

## Advertising

Nestblox displays ads provided by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

- Device identifiers (IDFA, when authorized via App Tracking Transparency)
- IP address (for approximate location-based ad targeting)
- App usage data (ad impressions, interactions)

You can opt out of personalized ads by declining the App Tracking Transparency prompt when the app first launches, or by adjusting your device settings under **Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking**.

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## In-App Purchases

Nestblox offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase that permanently disables banner and interstitial ads. This transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Nestblox does not collect or store any payment information.

## App Tracking Transparency

Nestblox requests your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before allowing any ad-related tracking. If you decline, only non-personalized ads will be shown. You can change this at any time in your device settings.

## Third-Party Services

Nestblox uses the following third-party services:

- **Google AdMob** — ad serving ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Apple StoreKit** — in-app purchases (handled entirely by Apple)
- **SKAdNetwork** — privacy-preserving ad attribution by Apple

## Children's Privacy

Nestblox does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is rated 4+ and does not serve targeted ads to users who have declined tracking.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
